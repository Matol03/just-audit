Trusted Partners

Just Audit is a professional audit and consulting firm operating across Kazakhstan. We conduct financial audits and provide tax consulting services in full compliance with International Standards on Auditing (ISA) and IFRS.

With offices in Astana, Almaty, and Semey, we serve clients across all sectors — from private enterprises to quasi-government organizations and members of the Astana Hub innovation ecosystem.
